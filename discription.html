Nestled in one of the most prestigious locations in the world, this Iconic Bungalow offers a lifestyle of unparalleled luxury and comfort. Situated near the legendary Burj Al-Arab, at the heart of the iconic Palm Jumeirah, this exceptional residence redefines opulence and exclusivity.

Boasting 4 spacious bedrooms, each designed with sophisticated style and attention to detail, this home provides the perfect balance of privacy and tranquility. The 3 elegant bathrooms are equipped with high-end fixtures and finishes, offering a spa-like experience in the comfort of your own home.
